Our Services

We provide compassionate and professional home care services tailored to meet the unique needs of each client. Our team is dedicated to ensuring the highest quality of life for our clients through personalized care plans, round-the-clock support and a range of services such as:

Companionship

This is done to make sure that we promote the mental and emotional health to ensure our clients maintain a sense of connection and engagement with the world around them. Hands of Peace companions ensure that the clients attend outings, monitor well-being and take necessary action in case of an emergency.

  • Providing social interaction
  • Give emotional support
  • And building stong relationships.

Homemaking

Here we aim to create a supportive environment that promotes independence, comfort and well-being for our clients. We have personal support workers and nurses that give care and support to people of all ages. The team works together to develop individualized care plans for our clients to make sure they enjoy stay in the communities.

  • We do the housekeeping and laundry
  • Run errands
  • manage and organize household items

Caregiver relief and Respite care

This is a form of temporary relief for primary caregivers to help them rest and recharge. It includes in-home respite, residential respite care and adult day centers. We provide support to caregivers, helping them sustain their lives.

  • We help relieve the caregiver of stress and burnout
  • We ensure that the caregiver receives time for self-care
  • We ensure peace of mind to the caregivers as they know the patient is in good hands.

Palliative Care

With this specialized form of medical care we focus on providing relief from the symptoms, pain and stress of any illness.Our goal is to improve the quality of health for both the patient and their family. This is done through coordiation of care, family support among others.

  • We address symptoms
  • Provide medical care
  • And also provide emotional support to the patient and their family.

Personal Care

At Hands of Peace, we aim at providing assistance with activities of daily living with joy. Assistance with daily activities such bathing, dressing and grooming. Our caregivers are always working to maintain the client's dignity, comfort and independence.

  • Assisting with prescribed exercises or physiotherapy routines
  • Provision of basic skin care to ensure overall skin health
  • Assistance with mobility around the home safely.

Medical Administration

This involves managing and administering medications and treatments to clients in their homes. We have professional caretakers who ensure timely and accurate medication administration and can handle emergencies. This is done to ensure that patients adhere to their prescribed medication to promote health and avoid complications.

  • Setting reminders to ensure medication is taken at the right time
  • Organizing dispensers to ensure the right medication quantity and dosage
  • Coordinating refills with pharmacies
